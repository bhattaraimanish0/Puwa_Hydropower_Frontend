Kiran Malla , Chairman

Mr. Kiran Malla has 37+ years of experience in hydropower sector.
He is the founder chairman of High Himalaya Hydro Construction Pvt. Ltd.
and multiple hydropower development companies like Puwa Khola – One
Hydropower Limited (Aayu Malun HPP – 21.00 MW), Union Mewa (23 MW),
Hongu Hydroelectric Company (28.9 MW) and Upper Tamor A (60 MW).
In his 37+ years of experience Mr. Malla has contributed in more than
50 hydropower projects in Nepal. Under his chairmanship, High Himalaya
Hydro Construction Pvt. Ltd. has completed more than 35 projects making
it No. 1 private hydropower Construction Company in Nepal. Academically,
Mr. Malla has completed bachelor’s in civil engineering from IIT, Roorkee and
Master in hydropower from Norway. He is the shareholder of multiple other
companies in Nepal and a successful professional and businessman.

Kadam KC , Managing Director

Kadam KC, an entrepreneur, with master’s in environmental science and Masters in
Geotechnical Engineering from UK universities, has been in business sector for more
than fifteen years. He has been involved in business and social works while was in the UK.
After coming back to Nepal, he is primarily working in hydropower and has successfully
completed Puwa Khola-1 Hydropower (4MW). He also chairs Aayu Entertainments Pvt. Ltd.,
which launching Fishtail Dream Park in Pokhara. He also has initiated a construction
company “Dhaulagiri Construction and Development Pvt. Ltd” and is also a board member
in High Himalaya Hydro Construction Pvt. Ltd.

Madhukar Garg, an entrepreneur, with Postgraduate in Electrical engineering specifically
“master’s in electrical Power Engineering” from NTNU (NORAD) and Kathmandu University
has extensively explored in reputed organisation like Hulas steel Industries. He is well versed
with technical parameter of hydropower. Being an electrical engineer he has extensive knowledge
on Electro-mechanical Equipment, Transmission line, etc., Presently, he has successfully completed
Puwa Khola-1 Hydropower (4MW) project and now aims to pursue his core interest -Hydropower
sector. He is primarily working in hydropower and is associated with various hydropower projects
which are under construction and in study phase.
